# Cinderfall Brand Lock

Date: 2026-04-14

Status: Locked for current project direction

## Decision

The project name is now:

`Cinderfall`

This replaces the previous Fireball Island working reference for all original project branding, documentation, and future implementation work.

## Why This Name

`Cinderfall` is short, punchy, easy to remember, and keeps the right sense of danger, verticality, and momentum without leaning on existing Fireball Island branding.

It also gives us room to build a distinct world and ruleset instead of feeling trapped in a one-to-one remake.

## What Stays

These core pillars are still in:

- A dramatic 3D island or mountain play space
- A race to claim a central objective and then escape
- Rolling environmental hazards that create chaos and pressure
- Minimal UI overlays
- Strong camera readability
- Robust, reusable, asset-ready systems

## What Changes

We are intentionally moving away from direct Fireball Island naming and presentation.

For now:

- Project name is locked as `Cinderfall`
- Direct legacy branding should not be used in future in-game naming
- Hazard naming is **not** locked yet
- Final turn structure is **not** locked yet

## Current Working Hazard Direction

The strongest current replacement direction is volcanic rolling hazards, for example:

- ember boulders
- volcanic bombs
- cinder stones

This keeps the physical drama of rolling threats while giving the game its own identity.

## Current Mechanics Direction

We are not locking the full rules yet.

The current preferred direction is a hybrid turn system:

- small hand of cards
- play 1 card per turn
- card grants movement and possibly a simple bonus or attack
- hazards remain a major board event, not just background noise

This keeps more drama and player agency than pure roll-and-move, without inheriting every oddity of the later Fireball Island revision.

## Next Documentation

The next useful report should define:

- final hazard naming
- central objective naming
- extraction/escape endpoint naming
- first playable turn loop
- starter card set

Until then, use `Cinderfall` as the project name in all new docs.
